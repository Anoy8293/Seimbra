1. My Profile
  • Parent can update their details in the My Profile Section.


  1. Screens

    2. Screen 1.1: Parent can find the My Profile in the left-hand side menu and click on it.

    Screen 1.2: Fill the required details.

    Screen 1.3: Fill the required details and click on Save Profile.




  2. Fields:
    • Personal Information:
      • First Name: Enter your first name.
      • Last Name: Enter your last name.
    • Contact Information:
      • School Email Address: Enter your child's school email address.
      • Mobile Number: Enter your mobile number.
      • Recovery Email Address: Enter a recovery email.
    • User Information:
      • Language Preference: Select your prefered language between engilsh and spanish [EN/ES].
      • Password: Enter your password.
      • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password.

  3. Procedure:
    • My profile section can be accessed from the Left-hand side menu on the dashboard screen.
    • In the My profile section, you can edit or update your information by filling the required fields.
    • Click on Save Profile once done.

  4. Notes:
    • Make sure your fill all the required details correctly.
    • The profile will be saved only when all the mandatory fields are filled.
    • You can go back to the user inquiry page at any time by clicking the “<” icon.

    Back To My Profile

7. My Colleges


  • In the "My Colleges" section, there are three dropdowns available: UC System, CSU System, and Private Colleges.
  • Parents can view the colleges along with direct link to their website provided.


  1. Screens

    2. Screen 7.1: My Colleges.


    Screen 7.2: UC, CSU and Private College Dropdowns.

    Screen 7.3: Individual Dropdown list of colleges with their respective links.



  2. Procedure:
    • In the "My Colleges" section, there are three dropdowns available: UC System, CSU System, and Private Colleges.
    • Clicking on the "+" icon opens the list of colleges under the selected system, displaying the names of the colleges along with their corresponding links.
    • Within each dropdown list, parents can select any college name. Clicking on a college name link redirects the parent to the respective college's web portal or site. This feature allows parents to quickly access information about specific colleges and explore their offerings.

  3. Notes:
    • Opening one dropdown automatically closes any previously opened dropdown, ensuring only one list is visible at a time.
    • You can go back to the user inquiry page at any time by clicking the “<” icon.

Back To My Colleges
