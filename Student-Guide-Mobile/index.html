1. My Profile
  • Student can update/merge progress in the My Profile Section.
  • If a student is not merged with an SFTP account then there are 2 ways to update their profiles
    • Left-hand side's Menu Bar
    • Dashboard "Update Profile" button


Screen 1.1: Student can find the profile in the left-hand side menu and click on it.



  • There are Four Parts of the My profile updating screen:

    • Personal Information.
    • Student Contact Information.
    • Parent/Guardian Information.
    • Student Academic Verification.

  1. Fields:

    • Personal Information Fields:

      • Checkbox: I would be the first in my family to attend college.
      • First Name: Enter your first name.
      • Last Name: Enter your last name.
      • Email address: Enter your personal email ID.
      • Language Preference Dropdown: Select your preferred language [EN/ES].
      • Choose File: Choose a file to upload a profile image.
      • Gender: Select the gender.
      • Birthday: Select the birthday.
      • Password: Enter your password.
      • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password.
      • Country Dropdown: Select your country.
      • State Dropdown/Input Field: Enter/select your state.

    • Student Contact Information Fields:

      • Mobile Number: Enter your mobile number.
      • Recovery Email Address: Add a recovery email address.

    • Parent/Gaurdian Contact Information:

      • Parent name: Enter your parent's name.
      • Parent email address: Enter your parent's email address.
      • Parent Mobile Phone Number: Enter your parent's mobile phone number.

    • Student Verification (2 methods):

      • With E-mail ID.
      • Without E-mail ID.


    1.1. With E-mail ID/Without Email ID



    1. Screens

      2. Screen 1.1.1: Fill the required details.

      Screen 1.1.2: Fill the required details.

      Screen 1.1.3: Fill the required details.

      Screen 1.1.4: In the verification type, Select With E-mail Id if you want to verify with E-mail Id.

      Screen 1.1.5: In the verification type, Select Without E-mail Id if you want to verify without E-mail Id.

      Screen 1.1.6: Enter the details required and enter the OTP received in your student E-mail ID.

      Screen 1.1.7: OTP verification Successful.

      Screen 1.1.8: Profile Update Successful.



    2. Fields:
      • For With Email ID:
        • School Email Address: Enter your school Email address.
      • For Without Email ID:
        • School Name: Enter your school's name.
        • Student ID: Enter your Student ID.

    3. Procedure:
      • Fill up the required details in the Personal Information, Student Contact Information and Parent/Guardian Information sections.
      • To view the Student Academic Information, you will first have to verify your account if registered with personal/google e-mail ID.
      • Select the With Email ID in the Student Academic Information for the Verification Type field.
      • Enter your School E-mail ID in the school E-mail Address field and Click on Verify Email.
      • If you have Selected Without Email ID then you will need to enter your School Name and your Student ID. After entering the required fields, you will be prompted with your school Email ID.
      • You will receive an OTP in your School Email ID. Enter the OTP to verify your account and access the academic information.
      • A pop-up message: "Profile Updated Successfully. To continue, please re-login using email below: ". Click on "OK".
      • You will be logged out and redirected to the Sign In page.
      • Sign In again using the E-mail ID prompted in the pop-up message.
      • Your goals and status would have been updated on the dashboard.

    4. Notes:
      • Make sure you fill all the required details correctly.
      • The profile will be saved only when all mandatory fields are filled.
      • The Verify Email button will be enabled only after the school's name and student ID is entered.
      • Click on "Resend OTP" if required.
      • Your Goal details would be visible only after the verification.
      • You can go back to the user inquiry page at any time by clicking the “<” icon.


    Back To My Profile

2.1 Verified Profile Goals
  • For a Verified Account, you can view your goals from the Left-Hand Side Menu.
  • The My Goals section in left-hand side menu consist of 3 sections:
    • CC Requirement
    • CSU Requirement
    • UC Requirement


    Screen 2.1.1: My Goals for Verified Account




  1. Screens

    • CC Goals

      • Screen 2.1.2: CC Goals

      Screen 2.1.3: CSU Goals

      Screen 2.1.4: UC Goals

      Screen 2.1.5: Click on Send Message to send a message to a counselor.

      Screen 2.1.6: Click on Change Goal to request your counselor to change your goal.



  2. Fields:
    • Progress Meter with Status Indicator: provides a graphic visualization of their progress.
    • View Details: View details of each individual goal.
    • Send Message: Send a message directly to the counselor regarding a goal.
    • Change Goal: Request a counselor to change the goal.

  3. Procedure:
    • In the My Goals section in the left-hand-side menu, Select the CC Requirements to view CC goals.
    • You will be redirected to the CC requirements dashboard.
    • Here, you can view your CC progress, as well as look at the details of each goal with the help of the View Details button.
    • With the help of the Send Message button, you can directly contact a counselor regarding any goal related queries.
      • On clicking this button, A "New Message" pop-up window is opened.
      • The pop-up window includes a dropdown menu to select a counselor.
      • In the input field, type the message.
      • Click on the Send Message button.
      • The Send message button is enabled only if a counselor is selected and a message is typed in the input field.
    • With the help of the Change Goal button, you can request a counselor to change a goal.
      • Specifically designed to send a Goal change request to a counselor.
      • On clicking this button, A "New Message" pop-up window is opened.
      • The pop-up window includes a dropdown menu to select a counselor.
      • In the input field, type the change request message.
      • Click on the Send Message button.
      • The Change Request button is enabled only if a counselor is selected and a message is typed in the input field.

  4. Notes:
    • If the Student clicks on the "View Details" button for a specific goal, the "Send Message" button dynamically changes its name to "Contact counselor".

4. My Messages
  • My messages feature enables the student to connect to their counselors, recruiters and School IT personnel.
  • There are 2 ways to access the messages:
    • From the Message Icon in the top right corner of the Dashboard Screen.
    • From the Left-Hand Side Menu bar, My Messages.


  1. Screens

    2. Screen 4.1: Accessing messages from the left-hand side menu from the My Messages.

    Screen 4.2: My Message screen, the message screen will open, displaying a list of names and preview messages from counselors, recruiters and School IT personnel.

    Screen 4.4: If user selects the Spanish [ES] Launguage, then the messages are displayed in Spanish.



  2. Notes:
    • Newly registered students cannot send messages to counselors, recruiters, or school IT personnel until they receive an initial message from them.
    • Upon selecting My Messages/Message Icon, the message screen will open, displaying a list of names and preview messages from counselors, recruiters and school IT personnel.
    • The message language is displayed based on the language selected in the profile section [EN/ES].
    • The language can be changed from My Profile section.
    • A search bar is provided within the Message screen for easy searching based on sender names.
    • When a new message is received, it appears at the top of the message list, with a message count displayed to the sender.
    • The Dashboard Message icon also displays the message count if a new message is received, providing a visual indicator of unread messages.
    • If a new message arrives while the application is open, a popup message will be displayed to notify the user instantly.

5. My Reflections
  • My reflections feature enables the student to fill the basic/UC/CSU class remediation.

  1. Screens

    2. 5.1: Students can find the my reflections tab in the side panel

    Screen 5.2: My Reflections screen, displaying the Basic Graduation Requirements..

    Screen 5.3: My Reflections screen, displaying the UC/CSU Eligibility.b.

    Screen 5.4: My Reflections screen, displaying the Questionnaire.

  1. Fields
    • Basic Graduation/UC/CSU Requirements:
    • First class that needs remediation:
      Students can enter the class name. After typing the third character, suggestions will appear, and the student can click on the specific class that requires remediation.
    • How do you plan to fix this:
      Students will select a single option from the dropdown menu. The purpose of this selection is to indicate how the student will be able to complete the class.
    • When do you complete this:
      Students will select a single option from the dropdown menu. The purpose of this selection is to indicate when the student expects to complete the class.
    • Additional Classes:
      In the same way student can fill the second class,third class sections.
    • Your current post-high school plans:
      Student enter the post high school plan.
    • How are you doing in your classes:
      Student expain the progress of the classes.
    • What can we do to help you:
      Student can enter the any help required related to class.
    • What can we do to help your success:
      Students can enter suggestions for better ways to achieve success.
    • Are you having any difficulties in your classes:
      Students can describe any difficulties they are experiencing in their classes.
    • How can your advisior better help you in your current challenges:
      Students can explain how their advisor can assist them more effectively with their current challenges.
    • What are your immediate top three goals for this semester:
      Students can enter their top three goals for the current semester.
    • Save Button:
      Saves updates or changes made to the data.
    • Cancel Button:
      Discards updates or changes made in the data.

  2. Procedure
    • Section I: Basic Requirements
      Fill in the basic requirements.
      Begin with the first class that needs remediation.
      Provide details on how and when you plan to complete this class.
      Fill in the details for the second and third classes that need remediation.
    • Section II: UC/CSU Requirements
      Fill in the UC/CSU remediation details for the first class that needs remediation.
      Provide the UC/CSU remediation details for the second class that needs remediation.
      Enter the UC/CSU remediation details for the third class that needs remediation.
    • Section III: Questionnaire
      If needed, complete the questionnaire in this section.
    • Saving and Canceling Changes:
      Click on "Save" button to save any changes or updates made to the data within the My Reflections section.
      Clicking on the "Cancel" button allows the student to discard any changes or updates made to the data within the My Reflections section

7. My Colleges

  1. Screens

    2. Screen 7.1: My Colleges

    Screen 7.2: UC, CSU and Private College Dropdowns.

    Screen 7.3: Individual Dropdown list of colleges with their respective links.



  • Procedure
    • In the "My Colleges" section, there are three dropdowns available: UC System, CSU System, and Private Colleges.
    • Clicking on the "+" icon opens the list of colleges under the selected system, displaying the names of the colleges along with their corresponding links.
